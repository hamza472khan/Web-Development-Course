The Importance of Sleep

Written by Dr. Smith

Getting enough sleep is very important for your health. It helps your body recover and stay focused.

A doctor once said:

Sleep is not a luxury. It is a necessity for the human body and mind.

During deep sleep, we experience the REM stage, which is essential for brain development and memory processing.

Experts say we need at least 6 8 hours of sleep every night.
